# SENRA Data Sources

All scores are computed from 2023–24 government data unless otherwise noted.

## Road Infrastructure
- **Source:** Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (MoRTH), Annual Report 2023–24
- **Metric:** National Highway length (km) per 1,000 sq km of state area
- **URL:** https://morth.nic.in/annual-report
- **Notes:** State area figures from Census 2011 (no revision since)

## Distributor Density
- **Source:** Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) — Udyam/MSME registration portal, wholesale/distribution NIC codes
- **Metric:** Active registered wholesale/distributor businesses per 1,00,000 population
- **URL:** https://udyamregistration.gov.in
- **Notes:** Captures formal economy only. Informal distributors not included.

## Monsoon Disruption Risk
- **Source:** India Meteorological Department (IMD), Rainfall Statistics of India 2023
- **Metric:** Composite of (a) coefficient of variation of annual rainfall 2000–2023, (b) average annual flood-affected area as % of state area (NDMA data)
- **URL:** https://imd.gov.in/pages/rainfall_main.php
- **Notes:** Drought risk not captured. Climate trend adjustment not applied.

## Logistics Access (LEADS)
- **Source:** Ministry of Commerce & Industry, LEADS 2023 Report
- **Metric:** LEADS composite score 0–100 (port efficiency, warehousing, multimodal, regulatory environment)
- **URL:** https://commerce.gov.in/publications
- **Notes:** LEADS 2023 is the most recent published edition as of data collection.

## Power Grid Reliability
- **Source:** Central Electricity Authority (CEA), Annual Report 2023–24
- **Metric:** Consumer-hours interrupted (CHI) per consumer per year, state-wise
- **URL:** https://cea.nic.in/annual-report
- **Notes:** Figures are utility-reported; actual outage duration may differ from consumer experience.

## Cold Chain Infrastructure
- **Source:** National Centre for Cold-chain Development (NCCD), State-wise Cold Storage Capacity 2023
- **Metric:** Metric tonnes (MT) of cold storage capacity per lakh population
- **URL:** https://nccd.gov.in
- **Notes:** Captures licensed cold storage only. On-farm storage not included.

## Distributor Concentration
- **Source:** Derived from MCA district-level business registration data
- **Metric:** Herfindahl-style concentration index — sum of squared district shares of wholesale business registrations within each state
- **Notes:** Proxy only. Measures geographic concentration of registered businesses, not actual logistics network topology.
